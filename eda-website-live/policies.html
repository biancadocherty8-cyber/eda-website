Privacy Policy

Exceptional Dental Academy ("EDA", "we", "us") respects your privacy and handles personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles.

What we collect

We collect information you provide directly, your name, email address, phone number and professional details when you subscribe to our newsletter, enquire, or enrol in a course. Course bookings and payments are processed by Eventbrite, who collect and handle your payment information under their own terms.

Cookies & tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies, including the Meta (Facebook) Pixel and analytics tools, to understand how the site is used and to measure and improve our advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

How we use your information

  • To deliver the courses and services you've requested
  • To send you course information, updates and marketing (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To improve our website, content and advertising

Who we share it with

We use trusted third-party providers to run our business, including Eventbrite (bookings & payments), Klaviyo (email), Meta and Google (advertising & analytics). We do not sell your personal information.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in any email. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

Refund & Cancellation

We understand plans change. Our cancellation terms for course bookings are:

  • More than 30 days before the course: full refund.
  • 15–30 days before the course: 50% refund, or transfer your place to a future cohort (subject to availability).
  • Within 14 days of the course: non-refundable, though you may transfer your place to another dentist.

To cancel or transfer, contact us as early as possible using the details below. Refunds are processed back to your original payment method via Eventbrite.
