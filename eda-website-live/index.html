Frequently asked

Everything you might be wondering.

Who are these masterclasses for?
General dentists who want to integrate predictable aesthetic dentistry (composite veneers, porcelain veneers, or full-mouth rehab) into their daily practice. We assume clinical experience. We don't assume aesthetic experience.
How hands-on is "hands-on"?
Genuinely hands-on. Every course is structured around live patient cases, phantom-head work, or both. You leave with skills your hands already know. Not just a folder of slides.
How big are the cohorts?
Capped tight. Typically 20 to 30 dentists per cohort, with multiple educators on the floor. That ratio is the whole point: you get direct feedback from clinicians who do these cases every week.
Is there support after the course?
Yes. Every graduate gets ongoing access to a private group, our case-review channel, and direct line to the educators for cases you're planning. The course is the start, not the end.
Do you offer CPD credit?
All courses are AHPRA-aligned and carry CPD hours. You'll receive a certificate of attendance after each course detailing CPD hours and learning outcomes.
